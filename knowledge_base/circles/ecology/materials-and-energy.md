## Roles
**Systems Abundance Analyst** - Monitors and models resource flows to ensure the community lives within regenerative means and understands true ecological surplus.  
**Soil Life Tender** - Focuses on rebuilding topsoil, fostering microbial life, and managing fertility through composting and regenerative practices as the foundation of all life.  
**Urban Miner** - Specializes in the careful recovery and recycling of metals and minerals from the built environment, minimizing the need for new extraction.  
**Micro-Grid Steward** - Manages the local, decentralized network of renewable energy sources (solar, wind, micro-hydro) to ensure resilience and equitable distribution.  
**Post-Carbon Transition Coordinator** - Manages the phased, just transition away from legacy fossil fuels, overseeing their reserved use for essential purposes only.  
**Technical Nutrient Manager** - Oversees the industrial cycle, ensuring all non-biodegradable materials are designed and managed as "food" for new production cycles.

## Critical Protocol
**"New Material/Energy Source Adoption" Protocol**  
**Purpose:** To govern the high-stakes decision of introducing a new source of material or energy into the community's metabolism.

**Process:**  
1. Full-System Impact Proposal: Lifecycle analysis detailing availability, energy inputs, and end-of-life plan
2. Multi-Generational Cost-Benefit Analysis: Modeling long-term ecological, social, and energy costs and benefits
3. Community-Wide "Metabolic Impact" Hearing: Public forum presenting analysis and addressing impacts
4. Provisional Adoption with Sunset Clause: Time-bound adoption with strict monitoring and stewardship bond
5. Full Integration or Phased Sunset: Decision based on performance data to integrate, modify, or phase out

**Implementation Toolkits:**  
- "Energy Descent & Resilience" Protocol Suite: Grid stress response manager, new technology integration framework, and community energy rationing system
- Circular Material Management Toolkit: Lifecycle assessment calculator, waste classification software, and material health screening

## Key Artifact
**Annual "Metabolism Report"**  
The community's comprehensive physical "check-up," quantifying its resource health and progress toward a closed-loop, renewable energy-based economy.

**Contents:**  
- The Circularity Index: Percentage of materials sourced from circular flows versus linear flows
- Energy Sovereignty Dashboard: Data on percentage of energy from renewables and progress on post-carbon transition
- Resource Health Assessment: State of key resource pools including soil organic matter levels and mineral stocks
- Metabolic Rift Analysis: Assessment of gap between consumption and region's regenerative capacity

**Beauty:** This artifact represents a profound reconciliation between human society and the planet's material flows. It tells the story of a community that has stopped seeing the world as a mine and a dump, and has started seeing it as a garden and a library of materials to be carefully stewarded.

**Generation Systems:**  
- Annual "Metabolism of Energy & Matter" Report System: Energy sovereignty index, circularity quotient for materials, embodied energy ledger, and innovation spotlight
