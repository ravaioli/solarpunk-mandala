## Roles
**Watershed Steward** - Holds primary responsibility for the health of the entire local watershed, from headwaters to aquifer, ensuring its vitality for all life.  
**Water Purification Guardian** - Maintains and innovates natural and technical systems for ensuring clean, safe drinking water for the community and ecosystem.  
**Airshed Guardian** - Monitors air quality and leads initiatives to maintain pristine air through transportation policy, greening, and industrial process design.  
**Microclimate Regenerator** - Uses strategic planting, water features, and earthworks to create beneficial local microclimates that buffer extreme weather.  
**Carbon Sequestration Gardener** - Implements and monitors strategies (afforestation, soil building, biochar) to actively draw down atmospheric carbon.  
**Climate Resilience Planner** - Develops and drills strategies for the community to adapt to unavoidable climate impacts, focusing on social and ecological solidarity.

## Commons

## Critical Protocol
**"Elemental Purity Breach & Response" Protocol**  
**Purpose:** To guide the community's immediate and long-term response to a critical contamination event in either the air or water system.

**Process:**  
1. Immediate Detection & Community Alert: Sensor network triggers automatic alert and verification
2. Source Containment & Exposure Prevention: Immediate containment of contamination source and direction of people away
3. Emergency Resource Activation: Coordination of alternative water supplies and distribution of protective equipment
4. Bioremediation & System Flush: Leading remediation effort using plants, fungi, or biological agents
5. Systemic Reform & "Sacred Pact" Renewal: Community process to identify root cause and implement binding changes

**Implementation Toolkits:**  
- "Drought & Deluge" Resilience Protocol Suite: Water scarcity response manager, flash flood mitigation planner, and air quality crisis response system
- Hydrological & Atmospheric Stewardship Toolkit: Water footprint analyzer, watershed health simulator, and pollution source identification matrix
