## Roles
**Mycoremediator / Phytoremediator** - Uses fungi and plants to actively clean up contaminated soil and water, healing the scars of the old industrial era.  
**Zero-Waste Systems Designer** - Designs and manages the community's system for reusing, repairing, and repurposing hard waste, aiming for zero landfill.  
**Nutrient Cycle Manager** - Oversees the system that treats human waste as a valuable resource, safely composting it for agricultural use.  
**Living Machine Tender** - Maintains constructed wetlands and other biological systems that treat effluent and stormwater naturally before it returns to the watershed.  
**Compost Master** - Manages the community's composting hubs, transforming all biological "waste" into high-value soil amendment.  
**Circular Economy Logistician** - Manages the logistics of collecting, sorting, and routing materials to the appropriate fabricators for recycling and remanufacturing.

## Commons
**Metabolic Rift Healing Commons**  
A transparent, community-wide platform that tracks, analyzes, and manages all of the community's waste streams and emissions with the ultimate goal of eliminating the very concept of "waste" by redesigning all outputs as inputs.

**Significance:** This Commons makes the community's metabolic "exhaust" visible and manageable. It is the critical tool for targeting cleanup, optimizing flows, and closing the loop on organic waste.

## Critical Protocol
**"Toxic Spill & Metabolic Crisis" Protocol**  
**Purpose:** To guide the community's immediate and long-term response to a serious pollution event or a major failure in a waste-processing system.

**Process:**  
1. Immediate Containment & Alert: First responders contain spills while alerting the entire community
2. Crisis Pod Mobilization: Entire Metabolic Healers Pod activates to manage cleanup logistics
3. Transparent Communication & Health Protection: Continuous updates and protection of drinking water and food systems
4. Remediation & Healing Phase: Community-wide effort to clean site using biological and mechanical means
5. Systemic Redesign & "Never Again" Mandate: Deep analysis of root cause and implementation of systemic changes

**Implementation Toolkits:**  
- Immediate Containment & Alert System: Step-by-step guide and digital alert system
- Crisis Pod Mobilization Manager: Digital war-room platform for task assignment and tracking
- Transparent Communication Template: Pre-prepared frameworks for providing updates during pollution events
- "Never Again" Mandate Facilitator: Structured process for root cause analysis and systemic changes

**Protocol Software:**  
- Zero-Waste Optimization Toolkit: Material Flow Analysis software, waste stream diagnostic kit, and circular procurement guide

**Contents:**  
- The Circularity Quotient: Percentage of material outputs successfully diverted from landfill/incineration
- Metabolic Health Indicators: Data on reduction of pollution levels in air, water, and soil
