## Roles
**Social Audit Facilitator** - Ensures all communal economic processes and resource flows are open to scrutiny and evaluation by any community member.  
**Complementary Currency Keeper** - Maintains and oversees any localized mediums of exchange designed to facilitate trade without enabling wealth hoarding.  
**Use-Value Cataloguer** - Documents and makes accessible the available goods and services within the community, focusing on their utility and embedded labor.  
**Commons Land Trustee** - Holds land and key assets in trust for the community, granting rights of use but not ownership or speculative sale.  
**Alienation Analyst** - Works to identify and redesign any tasks that create feelings of powerlessness or meaninglessness, drawing from critiques of "bullshit jobs".  
**Voluntary Contribution Registrar** - Tracks voluntary contributions to the commons fund based on ability, fostering a culture of generosity and collective responsibility.

## Commons
**Vital Signs Ledger**  
A unified, public-facing dashboard that serves as the community's central nervous system for economic and regulatory data. It integrates all flows of value, not just financial, into a single transparent interface.

**Significance:** This Commons is the bedrock of trust in the economic system. It makes the abstract concept of "the economy" into a tangible, legible, and manageable system. It is essential for preventing corruption and ensuring the system works for all.

## Critical Protocol
**"Resource Scarcity & Allocation" Protocol**  
**Purpose:** To manage the high-stakes scenario where a critical common resource faces unsustainable demand or acute shortage. This protocol prevents hoarding, conflict, and ensures a fair, transparent, and democratic response.

**Process:**  
1. Scarcity Declaration & Transparency: Formal declaration with full data on available supply and current demand
2. Community Deliberation on Values: Public forums to decide core values for allocation (need, community benefit, historical access, lottery)
3. Proposal & Impact Assessment: Brainstorming allocation methods with social, economic, and ecological impact assessment
4. Democratic Selection & Implementation: Democratic process selecting allocation method with tracking system
5. Post-Allocation Review & System Redesign: Review of process and proposal of long-term systemic solutions

**Implementation Toolkits:**  
- "Resource Scarcity & Allocation" Protocol Suite: Scarcity declaration system, community values deliberation platform, impact assessment matrix, democratic selection engine, post-allocation review system
- Regulatory Evolution Toolkit: Policy impact tracker, stakeholder analysis tool, regulatory experimentation framework, compliance monitoring system

## Key Artifact
**Annual "Integrity & Equity Audit"**  
A comprehensive and brutally honest assessment of the health, fairness, and transparency of the community's economic and regulatory systems.

**Contents:**  
- The Transparency Index: Metrics on data accessibility, participation in audits, and speed of resolving regulatory questions
- Flow Equity Analysis: Data visualizing distribution of goods/services, labor opportunities, and access to land/property
- Alienation & Fulfillment Report: Analysis of work quality, identifying roles with high dissatisfaction and celebrating successful redesigns
- Regulatory Evolution Timeline: Showcase of how taxes and regulations have been adapted to better serve common good

**Beauty:** This artifact embodies the principle that sunlight is the best disinfectant. It is a living narrative of a community's relentless pursuit of economic justice and systemic integrity.
