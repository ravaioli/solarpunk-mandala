## Roles
1. **Community Storyteller** - Curates and shares the ongoing narrative of the community, ensuring diverse voices and forms of expression are heard and valued.
2. **Signal Curator** - Filters the vast information landscape to provide the community with relevant, verified, and actionable news, free from addictive algorithms.
3. **Commons Translator** - Makes complex information accessible to all, regardless of ability or background, through plain language, audio, visual arts, and translation.
4. **Critical Thinking Mentor** - Teaches and facilitates methods for analyzing information, identifying biases, and forming well-reasoned, evidence-based opinions.
5. **Dissent Orchestrator** - Helps community members organize safe, effective, and creative forms of protest to challenge policies or norms constructively.
6. **Digital Sanctuary Guardian** - Implements and educates on tools and norms that protect personal data and communal spaces from surveillance and intrusion.

## Commons
- **Most Important Commons:** Knowledge and Media Commons
- **Commons Description:** A decentralized, open-access platform that serves as the primary repository for all community-generated and community-relevant information.
- **Commons Significance:** It is the lifeblood of an informed citizenry. Every role in this subdomain relies on it to disseminate information, access knowledge, facilitate dialogue, and hold power accountable. It prevents information silos, combats misinformation, and ensures the means of mental production are a common resource.

## Pod Structure
- **Core Pod Name:** Information Integrity Pod
- **Pod Mandate:** To ensure the community's information ecosystem is accurate, accessible, critical, and liberating. They are responsible for the health of public discourse.
- **Pod Composition:** Combines all 6 roles: Signal Curator, Critical Thinking Mentor, Commons Translator, Dissent Orchestrator, Digital Sanctuary Guardian, and Community Storyteller.
- **Pod-Specific Workspaces:** Information Integrity Pod Digital Headquarters with Collaborative Curation Platform, Critical Thinking Resource Hub, Accessibility Management System, and Dissent Coordination Space.
- **Pod Resource Kit:** Signal Curation Kit, Critical Thinking Toolkit, Accessibility Creation Kit, Digital Privacy Kit, and Storytelling Kit.

## Protocols
- **Critical Protocol Name:** Community Moderation and De-escalation Protocol
- **Protocol Purpose:** To address the recurring, high-stakes challenge of harmful speech, misinformation, and intense conflict in public forums.
- **Protocol Process:** 
  1. Flagging: Standardized system for flagging content
  2. Rapid Response: Assessment and temporary quarantine
  3. Restorative Dialogue: Facilitated dialogue between parties
  4. Community Review: Panel review with pod advice
  5. Resolution & Learning: Focus on education and reconciliation
- **Protocol Implementation Toolkits:** Community Moderation & De-escalation Protocol Suite including Harmful Speech Assessment Tool, Restorative Dialogue Facilitator, Community Review Panel Manager, Learning Case Anonymizer, and Escalation Pathway Navigator.
- **Protocol Software:** Information Integrity Toolkit including Verification Workflow Manager, Bias Detection Matrix, Accessibility Compliance Checker, and Algorithm Transparency Dashboard.
