## Roles
1. **Living Charter Steward** - Keeps the community's core principles alive in everyday discourse and ensures its protocols are living documents, updated by the community.
2. **Social Contract Facilitator** - Helps the community explicitly define and agree upon the mutual responsibilities required to maintain their shared project.
3. **Ethical Culture Gardener** - Models and fosters discussions about integrity, encouraging the community to live by its highest values.
4. **Witness** - Attends community processes as an official observer to ensure actions align with stated values and to call out discrepancies with compassion.
5. **Creative Tension Harvester** - Helps the community frame ideological disagreements not as threats, but as a source of creative energy and resilience.
6. **Consequences & Closure Facilitator** - Guides processes that determine fair consequences for broken agreements and facilitates ceremonies of closure and acquittal.

## Protocols
- **Critical Protocol Name:** "Breach of Charter" Accountability Protocol
- **Protocol Purpose:** To address the most critical ethical failure: a significant violation of the Community Charter by a person in a trusted role.
- **Protocol Process:** 
  1. Initiation & Disclosure: Filing and logging "Charter Concern"
  2. Community Tribunal: Diverse, randomly selected panel convened
  3. Truth & Impact Assessment: Articulating harm and acknowledgment
  4. Restorative Sanctioning: Co-creating "Restorative Sanction"
  5. Closure & Reaffirmation: Public ceremony for reintegration
- **Protocol Implementation Toolkits:** "Breach of Charter" Accountability Navigator with Charter Concern Intake System, Community Tribunal Randomizer, Restorative Sanction Designer, Closure Ceremony Planner, and Impact Assessment Toolkit.
- **Protocol Software:** Ethical Tension Harvesting System including Creative Tension Mapper, Values Conflict Resolver, Stakeholder Impact Simulator, and Multi-Generational Ethics Calculator.
