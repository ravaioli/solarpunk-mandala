## Roles
1. **Community Recognition Weaver** - Facilitates processes that acknowledge contributions and resolve disputes, building mutual respect outside a hierarchical chain of command.
2. **Initiative Catalyst** - Identifies community needs and empowers working groups to form around them, fostering agency without centralized command.
3. **Long-Term Imagination Steward** - Holds space for intergenerational visioning, using tools like back-casting to align short-term projects with long-term communal goals.
4. **Flow Coordinator** - Manages the logistics of resource distribution and meeting schedules, not to control, but to ensure smooth, transparent communal processes.
5. **Mandate Facilitator** - Ensures that any temporary authority (e.g., for a project) is granted by and remains accountable to the community assembly.
6. **Information Liberator** - Works to make all communal data‚Äîfrom resource stocks to decision logs‚Äîaccessible, understandable, and open to all.

## Commons
- **Most Important Commons:** Community Mandate & Memory Commons
- **Commons Description:** A dynamic, living repository that serves as the single source of truth for the community's self-governance.
- **Commons Significance:** Every role in Organization and Governance needs to interact with this Commons to ensure their actions are legitimate, aligned with the community's vision, and transparent. It prevents duplication of effort, power creep, and historical amnesia.

## Pod Structure
- **Core Pod Name:** Governance Integrity Pod
- **Pod Mandate:** To ensure the community's governance systems are functional, transparent, aligned with our values, and accessible to all members.
- **Pod Composition:** Combines all 6 roles: Community Recognition Weaver, Initiative Catalyst, Long-Term Imagination Steward, Flow Coordinator, Information Liberator, and Mandate Facilitator.
- **Pod-Specific Workspaces:** Governance Integrity Pod Digital Headquarters with Mandate Management Dashboard, Process Coordination Platform, Cross-Pod Alignment Monitor, and Capacity Balancing System.
- **Pod Resource Kit:** Facilitation Toolkit, Decision-Making Kit, Transparency Kit, and Initiative Launch Pack.

## Protocols
- **Critical Protocol Name:** Consent-Based Decision Making Protocol
- **Protocol Purpose:** To guide the community through the recurring, high-stakes process of ratifying the Annual Community Plan & Resource Apportionment.
- **Protocol Process:** 
  1. Submission: Governance Integrity Pod submits integrated Annual Plan
  2. Clarity & Consultation: Period for discussion and accessibility
  3. Consent Rounds: Structured process calling for consent
  4. Integration of Objections: Working with reasoned objections
  5. Ratification & Mandate: Plan becomes official mandate for the year
- **Protocol Implementation Toolkits:** "Consent-Based Decision Making" Navigator with Proposal Development Wizard, Stakeholder Mapping Tool, Objection Integration System, Mandate Tracking Dashboard, and Meeting Facilitator Assistant.
- **Protocol Software:** Governance Process Optimizer including Process Efficiency Analyzer, Participation Equity Monitor, Decision Quality Assessor, and Governance Health Dashboard.
