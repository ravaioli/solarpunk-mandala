## Roles
1. **Present-Moment Anchor** - Guides practices that help individuals and groups return to the here-and-now, reducing anxiety about the future and rumination on the past.
2. **Meditation Practice Guide** - Instructs in and holds space for various forms of meditation (stillness, movement, loving-kindness) as core life skills.
3. **Voluntary Simplicity Mentor** - Supports individuals in exploring what "enough" truly is, and finding freedom and focus through letting go of excess.
4. **Somatic Ambassador** - Leads practices (yoga, tai chi, authentic movement) that help people inhabit their bodies as sites of wisdom and spiritual insight.
5. **Prayer & Invocation Holder** - Maintains the forms and languages of prayer, invocation, and setting intention as ways to focus collective will and gratitude.
6. **Living Text Interpreter** - Facilitates the study of ancient and modern wisdom texts, making them relevant to the community's current ethical and spiritual challenges.

## Commons

## Pod Structure
- **Core Pod Name:** Practice Guardians Pod
- **Pod Mandate:** To inspire, support, and hold the container for the community's commitment to regular spiritual practice and disciplined living, recognizing that sustained effort is the bridge between inspiration and transformation.
- **Pod Composition:** Combines all 6 roles: Present-Moment Anchor, Meditation Practice Guide, Voluntary Simplicity Mentor, Somatic Ambassador, Prayer & Invocation Holder, and Living Text Interpreter.

## Protocols
- **Critical Protocol Name:** "Practice Commitment & Renewal" Protocol
- **Protocol Purpose:** To guide the community through the inevitable challenge of maintaining Discipline when motivation wanes, life becomes difficult, or practices feel dry and empty.
- **Protocol Process:** 
  1. Honest Assessment & Non-Judgmental Awareness: Community-wide check-in about struggles
  2. "Practice Pod" Support Circles: Small groups for sharing challenges and peer support
  3. Skillful Adaptation & "Practice Prescription": Adapting practices to individual needs
  4. Recommitment Ceremony: Formal recommitment to Discipline
  5. Integration of the "Shadow" of Practice: Working with resistance skillfully
- **Protocol Implementation Toolkits:** "Plateau & Stagnation Navigation" Protocol Suite including Plateau Diagnosis Guide, Practice Deconstruction & Rebuilding Toolkit, "The Grind" Support System, and Breakthrough Integration Ceremony.
- **Protocol Software:** Discipline & Accountability Toolkit including Commitment Contract Builder, Peer Review & Feedback System, and Master-Apprentice Agreement Templates.
