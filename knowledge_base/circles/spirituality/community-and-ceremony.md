## Roles
1. **Rites-of-Passage Weaver** - Designs and holds meaningful rituals for life's transitions (birth, adulthood, death) to provide container and meaning for change.
2. **Collective Trauma Alchemist** - Holds space for the community to safely process and transform shared historical or contemporary wounds through ceremony and witness.
3. **Living Myth Weaver** - Co-creates new, empowering myths and stories with the community that reflect its values and guide its evolution into the future.
4. **Temenos Gardener** - Designs, builds, and tends to physical spaces (groves, rooms, gardens) dedicated solely to quiet contemplation, ritual, and connection.
5. **Resonance Guide** - Uses voice, rhythm, and song to facilitate experiences of collective effervescence and unity, where individual boundaries soften.
6. **Cyclical Nourishment Host** - Orchestrates communal feasts to celebrate abundance and guides periods of collective fasting to cultivate focus and appreciation.

- **Protocol Purpose:** To guide the community through the high-stakes, inevitable experience of collective loss‚Äîwhether the death of a beloved member, a shared failure, or a community-wide crisis.
- **Protocol Process:**
