## Roles
1. **Vocation Sounding Board** - Helps individuals listen for their "calling" and find the unique way they are meant to contribute to the healing of the world.
2. **Story-of-Redemption Holder** - Helps individuals and the community reframe past failures and sufferings as sources of strength, wisdom, and eventual redemption.
3. **Archetype Explorer** - Teaches about universal archetypal patterns (Hero, Caregiver, Trickster) to help people understand their lives and the community's journey.
4. **Symbolic Literacy Teacher** - Helps the community develop fluency in reading the symbolic and metaphorical layers of reality in art, dreams, and world events.
5. **Active Hope Catalyst** - Moves beyond naive optimism to facilitate practices (like the Work That Reconnects) that build resilience and empower action for a positive future.
6. **Seven-Generation Thinker** - Guides the community to consider the impact of its decisions on the seventh generation to come, making legacy a living practice.

## Protocols
- **Critical Protocol Name:** "Crisis of Meaning & Narrative Repair" Protocol
- **Protocol Purpose:** To guide the community through the high-stakes situation when its shared story breaks down‚Äîwhen a foundational narrative is challenged, a shared purpose is lost, or hope is depleted.
- **Protocol Process:** 
  1. Narrative Diagnosis: Identifying where and why the community's story is failing
  2. Truth & Reconciliation with the Past: Honestly examining community history
  3. Purpose Rediscovery: Reconnecting with personal sense of purpose
  4. New Narrative Emergence: Collectively imagining new possible stories
  5. Ritual of Narrative Renewal: Formal transition to new narrative
- **Protocol Implementation Toolkits:** "Narrative Conflict & Integration" Protocol Suite including Narrative Explication Guide, Multiple Perspective Mapper, Integrative Story Weaving Facilitator, and Ritual of Narrative Release.
- **Protocol Software:** Narrative Foresight & Sense-Making Toolkit including Narrative Pattern Detector, Speculative Narrative Sandbox, and Metaphor Evolution Tracker.
