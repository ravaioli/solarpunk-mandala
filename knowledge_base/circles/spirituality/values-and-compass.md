## Roles
1. **Heart-Strengthener** - Mentors and supports individuals in finding the courage to speak and act according to their conscience, even when it is difficult.
2. **Compassion Practice Guide** - Teaches and facilitates practices (like metta meditation) to actively cultivate empathy and unconditional friendliness towards all beings.
3. **Wholeness Mentor** - Helps individuals and the community reconcile inner divisions and embody their values consistently, walking their talk.
4. **Moral Arc Advocate** - Keeps the principles of restorative justice and radical equity at the center of the community's spiritual conscience and social practice.
5. **Sacred Duty Reminder** - Frames work for the common good not as a chore, but as a sacred practice of stewardship and a joyful expression of interdependence.
6. **Right-Relation Guide** - Helps the community remember its proper scale within the broader web of life, countering hubris and the fantasy of human supremacy.

## Protocols
- **Critical Protocol Name:** "Ethical Dilemma Resolution" Protocol
- **Protocol Purpose:** To guide the community through the most challenging ethical conflicts, where core values such as Justice and Equity and Compassion and Empathy appear to be in direct tension, or when a significant breach of the community's Integrity and Wholeness occurs.
- **Protocol Process:** 
  1. Sacred Pause & Container Setting: Establishing container of deep listening
  2. Values Mapping & Tension Identification: Mapping conflicting values at play
  3. Compassionate Deliberation: Facilitating process where all parties speak to values
  4. Resolution Through Integration: Guiding toward resolution that integrates core values
  5. Reconciliation & Charter Reflection: Holding space for reconciliation and proposing clarifications
- **Protocol Implementation Toolkits:** "Values Conflict Navigation" Protocol Suite including The Principled Pause Facilitator's Guide, The Values Tension Mapping Process, The Ethical Impact Assessment Framework, and The "Return to Source" Ceremony Guide.
- **Protocol Software:** Compass Calibration Toolkit including Personal Values Clarification Engine, Decision-Making Lens Builder, and Intergenerational Values Bridge.
