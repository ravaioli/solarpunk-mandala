## Roles
1. **Intuition Cultivator** - Creates safe spaces and practices (like focusing, dreamwork) to help people trust and refine their inner knowing beyond rational thought.
2. **Inner Journey Guide** - Facilitates solo and group processes for deep self-inquiry, helping individuals understand their motivations, patterns, and inner landscape.
3. **Discernment Counselor** - Helps individuals and groups make choices aligned with their deepest values and the common good, distinguishing ego from essence.
4. **Silence Keeper** - Curates and protects spaces for quiet contemplation and solo retreats, understanding them as essential for psychological and spiritual health.
5. **Shadow Integration Facilitator** - Skillfully guides individuals and the community to acknowledge, befriend, and integrate repressed or denied aspects of the self and collective.
6. **Alignment Witness** - Acts as a mirror for individuals and the community, gently pointing out discrepancies between stated values and actions, fostering integrity.

## Commons
- **Most Important Commons:** Sanctuary of the Self Commons
- **Commons Description:** A protected, non-judgmental repository and resource hub dedicated to the inner journey. It is a curated collection of tools, prompts, and shared wisdom designed to support individual introspection without prescribing a single path or outcome.

## Pod Structure
- **Core Pod Name:** Inner Gardeners Pod
- **Pod Mandate:** To create and hold a community culture that values, respects, and supports the inner journey of each member. They are facilitators and space-holders, not gurus, who provide the conditions for inner wisdom to flourish.
- **Pod Composition:** Combines all 6 roles: Intuition Cultivator, Inner Journey Guide, Discernment Counselor, Silence Keeper, Shadow Integration Facilitator, and Alignment Witness.

## Protocols
- **Critical Protocol Name:** "Council of the Inner Voice" Protocol
- **Protocol Purpose:** To provide a structured, sacred container for an individual or the community to navigate a profound dilemma, a crisis of meaning, or a major decision where external advice is insufficient.
- **Protocol Process:** 
  1. Sacred Container Setting: Establishing physically and emotionally safe space
  2. Articulation & Release: Presenting the dilemma to fully release it into space
  3. Guided Descent into Silence: Prolonged period of silence using meditation techniques
  4. Harvesting Inner Wisdom: Journaling, drawing, or moving to express insights
  5. Integration & Embodiment: Formulating clear "next step" that aligns with insight
- **Protocol Implementation Toolkits:** "Inner Conflict & Shadow Integration" Protocol Suite including Somatic Awareness Anchor Guide, Journaling for Insight Process, Council of Selves Facilitator's Guide, and Integration Ceremony Designer.
- **Protocol Software:** Contemplative Technology & Practice Toolkit including Digital Mindfulness Reminder System, Practice Commitment Tracker, and Biofeedback & Inner Climate Monitor.
