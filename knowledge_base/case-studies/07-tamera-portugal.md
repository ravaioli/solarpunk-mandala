# Tamera Peace Research Village: Healing the Roots of Violence in Portugal's Alentejo

It engages in "peace research" as a core function, studying its own system to generate transferable knowledge.

## 1. Context & Initiative Profile

- **Location:** Alentejo region, Portugal.
- **Initiative Name:** Tamera Peace Research Village & Healing Biotope.
- **Year(s) of Observation/Study:** 1995 onward (from founding in Portugal).
- **Core Catalyzing Challenge(s):** The root causes of violence in human relationships and between humanity and nature. Founders asked: "How can we live without lying, without fear, and in truth with each other and the Earth?"
- **Brief Description:** A community of ~200 residents from over 20 nations engaged in "peace research," integrating radical social healing (truth in relationships, non-violent communication, sexual peace), ecological restoration (water retention landscapes), and spiritual practice to create a model for a non-violent, cooperative future.

## 3. Implementation Narrative

### Phase 1: Foundational Intention & Social Experimentation
Founded by theologian Dieter Duhm and others, Tamera began with a clear research question: "How do we build a community free of fear and lies?" Early years involved intense, often difficult social experimentation to develop tools for radical transparency ("no secrets"), trust-building, and transforming jealousy and power dynamics, particularly around love and sexuality ("Love School").

### Phase 2: Ecological Healing as Social Foundation
Recognizing that social peace is impossible under ecological scarcity, Tamera began the massive transformation of its 330-acre arid site. Following the "Plan of the Water" by Sepp Holzer, they built over **150 water retention ponds and lakes**, creating a self-sustaining water cycle. This restored the land, provided food security, and created tangible beauty, which in itself became a healing force for the community.

### Phase 3: Integration & Global Outreach
The community matured into a stable, resilient system where social tools, ecological practices, and spiritual inquiry reinforced each other. This allowed Tamera to focus on its research and educational mission, hosting thousands of visitors and running the "Hope" and "Terra Deva" schools to share its learnings on building "Healing Biotopes" worldwide.

## 4. Outcomes & Measurable Effects

- **Systemic Changes:** The creation of a durable, replicable model for a "Healing Biotope"—a defined area where ecology, social structure, and spirituality are coherently aligned toward peace.
- **Regenerative Capital Metrics:** Extraordinary levels of **Interbeing Capital** and **Presence Capital**. High **Regenerative** and **Biophilic Capital** in the landscape. **Relational Capital** is maintained through rigorous conflict transformation practices. **Wisdom Capital** is generated and disseminated.
- **Alpha (α) Coefficient Trend:** A high and stable Alpha score, reflecting profound systemic integration and balance. The community's explicit focus on healing "cubes" of separation and fear directly targets the Balance Penalty (B), optimizing for wholeness.
- **Tangible Outputs:** A restored, water-abundant ecosystem from degraded land. 30+ years of community life with **zero internal violence**. A body of published research and educational materials. International network of inspired projects.

## 5. Critical Analysis & SPM Insights

- **Key Learning:** **Ecological and social regeneration are two sides of the same coin.** You cannot heal land with traumatized people, and you cannot heal traumatized people on a starving, scarred land. The work must be simultaneous and integrated.
- **Tessera Connection:** Tamera provides the master tile for the **SPIRITUALITY Circle** within the SPM. It demonstrates that Spirituality is not an add-on but the foundational **quality of consciousness** (love, trust, truth) from which regenerative politics, economics, and ecology must spring to be sustainable.
- **Boundary Pathology/Medicine Observed:** The core pathology addressed is universal **Fragmentation** (inner/outer, human/human, human/nature). The **Boundary Medicine** is total: creating **permeable, healing membranes** at every level—within the self through therapy, between people through dialogue, and with nature through symbiotic design.
- **Dialectical Tensions Navigated:** **Structure vs. Freedom / Individual Desire vs. Collective Harmony.** Tamera's "Political Art" and social agreements are not rigid rules but evolving structures designed to maximize authentic freedom and minimize covert power. They navigate this 3D+ tension through continuous, conscious communication and consent-based updates to their social contract.
- **Limitations & Blind Spots:** The model is extraordinarily demanding of time and emotional labor. Questions of scalability and economic resilience within the broader capitalist context remain. A metasystem critique might ask: Does the focus on creating a perfected "biotope" create a new boundary between Tamera and the "unhealed" world, and how is that navigated?
