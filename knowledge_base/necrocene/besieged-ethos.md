## Tetrad Analysis
- **Object of Perception**: Success/Leadership
- **ENHANCES**: Short-term competitive advantage; ruthless efficiency in extraction; manipulation skills
- **OBSOLESCES**: Empathy; cooperation; ethical constraints; long-term trust building
- **RETRIEVES**: Machiavellian prince and robber baron as cultural ideals
- **REVERSES INTO**: Pervasive distrust makes collaboration impossible; organizational psychopathy destroys institutional knowledge and resilience
