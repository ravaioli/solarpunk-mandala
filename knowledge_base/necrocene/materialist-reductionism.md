## Overview
The foundational belief that consciousness is an accidental byproduct of matter, rendering the universe fundamentally meaningless and mechanical. Reduces consciousness to matter to justify treating people, animals, and ecosystems as inert resources to be controlled and owned.

## Core Concepts
- **Mechanistic Governance**: Society as a machine to be optimized through control and surveillance
- **Resource Computation**: Value reduced to quantifiable metrics; living systems as inventory
- **Epiphenomenal Culture**: Art, meaning, and spirituality as evolutionary byproducts without inherent significance
- **Dead Nature**: Environment as interchangeable parts in a planetary machine
