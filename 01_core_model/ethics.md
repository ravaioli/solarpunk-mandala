# C. Ethical Commitments

Purpose
- Document ethical principles guiding design, contribution, and distribution.

Principles
- Sustainability-first: prefer materials, assets, and patterns that emphasize lower impact.
- Community consent and credit: document authorship and licensing for assets.
- Accessibility and inclusivity: ensure designs are legible and usable.

Practical policies
- Licensing defaults, asset vetting checklist, attribution format.
