# Multiple Intelligences Framework

The Mandala's strength lies in its capacity to integrate multiple forms of intelligence beyond conventional rational paradigms. Its geometric architecture creates space for diverse intelligences to coexist and inform each other.

## Established Intelligences Within Current Framework
1. **Embodied Intelligence:** Recognizes bodily wisdom as foundational through the four Embodied Foundations
2. **Material Intelligence:** Selects and works with materials that respond to local conditions (Section II.G)
3. **Boundary Intelligence:** Understands permeability as design principle rather than security flaw
