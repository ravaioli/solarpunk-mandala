*The phase of deep inquiry and collective insight.*

**Objective:** To convene the **Council of the Whole**, interpret the data, and identify the core "pattern to dissolve."

**Format:** A dedicated, facilitated 90-120 minute community meeting.

**Agenda:**
1.  **Opening (5 min):** Centering silence and reading of the dashboard's purpose.
2.  **Presentation (15 min):** The Dashboard Coordinator presents:
    *   The new **Regeneration Flower**, visually compared to the last cycle's.
    *   The new **Alpha (Î±) Coefficient** and its trend.
    *   Key highlights from the **Q data** (e.g., "We planted 15 new trees," "Time bank hours dropped by 20%").
3.  **Narrative Listening (30 min):** The Facilitator shares **anonymous themes** from the *narrative answers* of the poll. This is not about numbers, but about *stories and feelings*. (e.g., "Several people shared stories of feeling time-pressured," "Many noted the joy from the new mural").
4.  **Diagnostic Question (25 min):** The council breaks into small groups to discuss:
    > **"Looking at the Flower's shape, the Alpha score, and the stories we heardâ€”which single 'cube' (rigid, limiting pattern) is most asking to be dissolved to allow for greater life and flow in our community?"**
    *   Is it a **Productivity Cube** (low Convivial Capital)?
    *   Is it a **Transaction Cube** (low Relational Capital)?
    *   Is it a **Separation Cube** (low Interbeing Capital)?
5.  **Synthesis & Proposal (15 min):** Groups report back. The facilitator identifies the **strongest consensus candidate**. The council formally names the "cube" for this cycle (e.g., "We are diagnosing a **Separation Cube**, as seen in low Interbeing and Biophilic scores").

## Troubleshooting & Evolution

*   **Low Poll Participation:** If response rates are low, simplify the poll or host it in person as a mandatory part of a community meal. Emphasize that the narrative data is irreplaceable.
*   **Flat or Declining Alpha Coefficient:** This is a key signal. It suggests the community's interventions are not addressing core issues. Use the Ritual of Diagnosis to go deeper. Consider that the "cube" may be misdiagnosed.
*   **Lopsided Regeneration Flower:** A persistently unbalanced flower is the primary diagnostic tool. The community must consciously choose to invest energy in the lagging domains, even if it feels less "natural" or productive in the short term.
*   **Evolving the Dashboard:** After 3-4 cycles, the community should review the capital definitions and metrics. Do they still reflect what we value? **The dashboard is a living document and should evolve with the community's consciousness.**
