### Ekistics in Practice
When designing regenerative spaces, consider how all five elements interact:
- A community garden integrates **NATURE** (soil, plants), **MAN** (human needs), **SHELLS** (garden beds, tool storage), **NETWORKS** (water systems, seed sharing), and **SOCIETY** (cultural knowledge sharing).
- The most resilient settlements honor the intelligence of this spatial ecology rather than imposing rigid human designs upon it.
