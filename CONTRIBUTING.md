# Contributing

Quick guide
- Fork the repo and create a branch for your change: naming convention `topic/<short-description>`.
- Add content under the appropriate folder (01_core_model, 02_foundations, 03_tools_prompts).
- Use clear commit messages and a small PR description.

PR checklist
- [ ] Add or update docs as needed.
- [ ] Include licenses and attributions for external assets.
- [ ] Provide a short test or example when submitting code or prompts.

## On the Role of AI in Development

The Solarpunk Mandala is a human vision. Its foundational ontology (Analytic Idealism), core geometry (the Tesseract), ethical axes, and integrative purpose are the products of human philosophical inquiry, synthesis, and intention.

In its documentation and articulation, large language model (LLM) AI systems were employed as a **cognitive tool and collaborative accelerant**. Their primary functions were:

*   **Amplification & Articulation**: Assisting in expanding seed concepts into detailed, consistent explanatory text across a large documentation set.
*   **Systematic Cross-Pollination**: Helping to identify and draft connections between the Mandala's components and established transdisciplinary fields (e.g., cybernetics, cognitive science, living systems theory).
*   **Drafting & Structuring**: Generating initial drafts for sections, tables, and document outlines based on provided concepts and architectural direction, which were then critically revised, corrected, and deepened by the human author.

In this process, the AI functioned as a **dedicated research assistant and editor**. The human author remained the **architect, final arbiter of coherence, and source of all critical judgments and creative leaps**. The use of this tool reflects a modern approach to intellectual synthesis, where technology aids in managing complexity and expressing ideas, while the vision, responsibility, and depth of understanding remain fundamentally human.
