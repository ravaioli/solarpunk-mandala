## Case Study
Bhutan's Gross National Happiness Index (1972-present): Bhutan's alternative to Gross Domestic Product demonstrates biocentric measurements through assessment of meaningful political participation, well-being indicators including mental health and life satisfaction, cultural vitality preservation of traditional practices, and ecosystem health through constitutional environmental protection.
