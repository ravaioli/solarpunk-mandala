## Case Study
Ford Model T and Consumer Democracy (1910s-1920s): The introduction of affordable automobiles represents a pivotal moment when American Dreams of consumer culture penetrated rural and isolated areas. This demonstrates how "democracy of goods" was marketed as universal prosperity while creating new dependencies on industrial systems, debt structures, and resource extraction.
