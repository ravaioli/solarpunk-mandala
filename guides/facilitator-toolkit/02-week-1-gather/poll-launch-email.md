# Poll Launch Email Template

## Purpose & How to Use

This is a template for announcing the monthly qualitative Community Poll. You can adapt it for an email, a message in your group chat (Signal/Telegram), or as a post on your community board.

**Instructions:** Copy the text below. Fill in the bracketed `[ ]` information. Choose the options that fit your community's tone. Send it from a trusted account (e.g., the Dashboard Coordinator or Communications Link).
